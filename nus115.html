NUS 115
To commemorate NUS's 115th anniversary, we developed a champagne-themed website designed to highlight NUS's accomplishments throughout its history, while also featuring significant milestones. The website incorporates intricate SVG line animations to deliver an immersive web browsing experience, paying homage to this remarkable milestone.

We leveraged vanilla JavaScript and CSS to animate the movements of SVG lines. With more than 500 distinct lines, meticulous attention was given to measuring each line and calculating their movements to ensure a seamless and captivating user experience.

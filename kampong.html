The Youth Action Challenge (YAC) is where like-minded youths come together in teams to brainstorm and pitch ideas for a better future. Through the YAC, youth are equipped with skills and opportunities to innovate their solutions in partnership with the Government. Funding is available to help youths turn their ideas to reality.

Prokect Kampong is a Youth-led platform dedicated to connecting young Singaporean leaders to build social good initiatives, together!
We want to discover and connect talented and motivated individuals from all corners of society. Kampong! aims to provide tangible and intangible infrastructure and support to these Youths to solve problems effectively and in a sustainable manner.
